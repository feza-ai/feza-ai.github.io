Many is more

Feza isn't just another trading algorithm. We are a decentralized intelligence platform. Instead of one large, slow-to-adapt model, we deploy an entire extremely light-weight army of AI traders. Each one is a dedicated expert on a single stock, cryptocurrency, or asset, continuously learning from market data in real-time. This massively multi-model approach allows us to be more agile, adaptive, and resilient than traditional trading systems.

Designed for scale.

At the heart of Feza is Zerfoo, an open-source machine learning framework built in Go. Designed from the ground up for performance and scale, Zerfoo allows us to train and deploy thousands of models with an efficiency that off-the-shelf tools simply cannot match. By building our own core technology, we control our destiny and maintain a critical competitive edge.
